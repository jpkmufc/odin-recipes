Open-Faced Tuna Melt

Description

A quick, classic diner sandwich with an open-face twist.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sandwich bread, white or wheat
  • 1 can oil or water packed tuna
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1 or 2 leaves chruncy letture (can substitute arugula for a bit of a kick
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mixed, dried spices, such as onion powder, garlic powder, cayenne, paprika (smoked or sweet) etc.
  • 4 slices of your favorite cheese
Stepss
  1. Set rack 6 inches from broiler and turn broiler to high.
  2. Lighly toast sandwich bread.
  3. Open and drain the can of tuna and place in small bowl.
  4. Add mayonnaise and spices and mix thouroughly.
  5. Place lettuce on top of each slice of bread, followed by onion and tuna mix. Place Tomato on top of tuna and cheese on top of tomato.
  6. Place sandwiches under broiler until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 2 minutes.
  7. Remove and let cool slightly.
