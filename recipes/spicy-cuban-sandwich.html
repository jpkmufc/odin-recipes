Spicy Cuban Sandwich

Description

A tasty spin on the classic cuban sandwich, with chicken.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbls butter
  • 1 yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 6 in french baguette, cut in half length wise
  • 1 tbls mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sweet and spicy pickles
  • 4 tbls mustard
  • 4 slices deli ham
  • 4 slices swiss cheese
  • 4 thin slices mojo pork
Steps
  1. Heat a large skillet over medium high heat. When heated, place deli ham in skillet and lightly brown. Remove from skillet.
  2. Place mojo pork into pan and cook until heaviy browned. Remove from pan.
  3. Add 2 tbls butter to skillet. When melted and bubbly, place onion into skillet and cook until softed and lighly browned, about 5 minutes.
  4. Spread 1 tablespoong of mayonnaise and 1 tablespoon of mustard on each half of the baguette. Place alternating layers of swiss cheese, ham, pork, onions, and pickles on bread and closed. Push down on sandwhich to smash.
  5. Heat remaining 2 tablespoons of butter in skillet over medium-high heat. Place sandwhich in skillet and cover (helps to melt the cheese). Cook until bread is browned and crispy on both sides, about 2 or 3 minutes per side.
